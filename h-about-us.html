What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Five foundational principles that guide every decision, every shift, and every brand under our roof.

01
Customer Obsession
Every decision starts and ends with the customer. We obsess over their experience, comfort, and delight at every touchpoint.
02
100% Responsibility
We own our outcomes — the wins and the losses. Full accountability is not a policy; it's the culture we breathe.
03
Open & Honest Communication
Transparent dialogue at every level. We say what we mean, hear what is said, and act with integrity.
04
Job Scope = Strength + Interest
We place people in roles that align with their natural strengths and genuine passions — because the best work comes from the best fit.
05
Continuous Improvement
We never settle. Every process, every menu, every guest interaction is an opportunity to be better than yesterday.

Est. 1995

A Company Built on
Culture & Craft

Founded in 1995, RCX Group has grown from a single concept into one of Asia's most dynamic F&B houses — building brands with soul, brands that endure.

We are not just operators. We are storytellers, builders, and believers in the power of a great meal, a great night, and a great brand to change how people feel.

Whether it's a Michelin-recognised restaurant, a craft beer microbrewery, or an intimate tapas bar — every brand in our house exists because it deserves to exist.
